# 📱 Time-Wasters on Social Media Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Time-Wasters on Social Media Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Excel dashboard developed to analyze social media usage patterns, user demographics, content preferences, engagement behavior, and time consumption across multiple social media platforms.

The dashboard transforms raw social media data into meaningful insights through Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPIs, and interactive slicers, helping users understand digital consumption behavior and engagement trends.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze social media usage patterns.
- Measure total time spent across platforms.
- Identify the most engaging content categories.
- Compare social media consumption across countries.
- Understand user behavior by age and gender.
- Analyze viewing habits during different times of the day.
- Support data-driven content and marketing strategies.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Platform Performance
- TikTok recorded the highest total time spent.
- YouTube and Instagram showed strong engagement levels.
- Facebook generated the lowest overall usage.

### Geographic Analysis
- India recorded the highest social media consumption.
- The United States ranked second in total time spent.
- Germany showed the lowest overall usage.

### Demographic Analysis
- Female users contributed a larger share of total time spent.
- Younger age groups spent significantly more time on social media.

### Content Preferences
- Jokes & Memes generated the highest viewing time.
- Life Hacks and Gaming were among the most popular content categories.
- Comedy received the lowest total viewing time.

### User Behavior
- Evening hours recorded the highest social media activity.
- Night-time usage was also considerably high.
- Habit and procrastination were major reasons for social media consumption.
